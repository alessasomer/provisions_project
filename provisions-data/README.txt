
PROVISIONS DATA FOR ECON 370 FALL 2024

ECON370-provisions.transactions.csv contains data on cash register sales transactions between 2024-01-01 and 2024-06-30.

prep-transactions-data-2024-09-12.R was used to prepare the transactions data set.

ECON370-provisions-items.csv contains details of all the items that Provisions sells.

prep-item-data-2024-09-12.R was used to prepare this file.

These data sets may only be used for analysis related to ECON 370, and should not be shared with individuals who are not either employees of Provisions or participants in the class.
